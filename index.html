3D city modeling and BIPV analysis
AerialInspect leverages LOD-1 3D city models to assess and visualize the solar energy potential of building surfaces, helping users maximize Building Integrated Photovoltaic (BIPV) installations. It offers a detailed analysis of solar irradiation, energy generation, and rooftop solar panel placement, tailored to specific user needs. Future enhancements will include scenario planning tools, impact analysis dashboards, and API access for seamless integration with architectural systems.
